Welcome to our serene school and temple of monks, where wisdom and spirituality intertwine harmoniously. Nestled amidst nature's embrace, our institution serves as a sanctuary for seekers of knowledge and enlightenment. Immerse yourself in an atmosphere of tranquility, Our esteemed monks, embodying wisdom accrued through generations, impart profound teachings and guide you on your spiritual journey. Experience the profound power of mindfulness and meditation, as our dedicated monks offer guidance and support to nurture your inner growth. Engage in meaningful discussions and contemplative practices that cultivate a deep understanding of oneself and the world around us. Discover the transformative power of ancient rituals, as we honor and celebrate the sacred traditions passed down through centuries. With a curriculum rooted in mindfulness, compassion, and ethical values, our school fosters holistic development of the mind, body, and soul.

our mission

At our school and temple of monks, our mission is to create a sacred space where wisdom, spirituality, and education converge harmoniously.We are dedicated to nurturing the growth and development of individuals through ancient teachings, mindfulness practices, and ethical values. Through the wisdom of our esteemed monks, we aim to impart profound teachings that empower individuals to lead meaningful and purposeful lives. Our mission is to honor and preserve ancient traditions, rituals, and scriptures, ensuring their continued relevance in the modern world.We are committed to creating a vibrant community that supports and uplifts one another, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
